Why This Is Different

Most bond tools explain rates or curves in isolation. This engine is built around market structure: who may be buying, how flows may be shifting, and whether conditions remain orderly or increasingly fragile.

What It Is Designed For

The Flow & Positioning Engine is intended for macro monitoring, institutional interpretation and scenario analysis. It is not a black-box forecast, but a structured way of reading fixed income conditions.

Methodology

The engine combines simplified signal blocks across institutional demand support, flow pressure, auction-style stress, real yield pressure, cross-market yield conditions and liquidity-sensitive inputs.

These are translated into a broader bond market positioning regime: Risk-On, Neutral, Policy Transition, Defensive or Liquidity Stress.

This BondStats engine is provided for informational and educational purposes only. It is a simplified analytical framework and should not be interpreted as investment advice, regulatory analysis or an official market forecast.
